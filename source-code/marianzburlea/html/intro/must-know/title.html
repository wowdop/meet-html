Should be placed in the head tag

tag defines the title of the document. The title must be text-only, and it is shown in the browser's title bar or in the page's tab.

Search engines love a good title and a reason to visit your website

    <!-- the content inside the title tag is usually -->
    <!-- displayed as the big blue text link when using a -->
    <!-- search engine like Google -->
    <!-- and browsing through the search results -->
    <title>A problem or a solution</title>
    
    <!-- the value of the content attribute of the meta tag -->
    <!-- that has name="description" usually gets -->
    <!-- displayed as the normal black text, -->
    <!-- under the big blue text link when using a -->
    <!-- search engine like Google -->
    <!-- and browsing through the search results -->
    <meta 
      name="description" 
      content="This is a message that fits the reason to click and visit"
