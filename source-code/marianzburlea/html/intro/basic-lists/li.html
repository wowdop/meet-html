List items can be children of either ul or ol

Example:

List of things to remember

  • Pick up keys
  • Deliver parcel
  • Buy flowers
  • Eat cake

Another Example:

List of big planets

  1. Mercury
  2. Venus
  3. Earth
  4. Mars
  5. Saturn
  6. Neptune
  7. Uranus

Code:

     <p>List of things to remember</p>
     <ul>
      <li>Pick up keys</li>
      <li>Deliver parcel</li>
      <li>Buy flowers</li>
      <li>Eat cake</li>
     <ul>
