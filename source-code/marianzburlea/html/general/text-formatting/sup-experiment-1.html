Let's play around with sup

I will make use of blockquote as well.

sup helps you write characters above the normal line

Anne sent me a letter and it began like this: 
          1st of June 2022
          Anne Blake
          Manchester,
          UK

Hello, Darling.

I hope your studies go well. I am looking forward to see your new skills on the 15th of July at the competition. See you soon!
