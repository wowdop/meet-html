Time tag experiment

Below is an reminder about an event.

The London Eye will be reopened from July 7 .

Opening hours start at 09:00 and you'll be able to enjoy the view for at least 1h 30m per ticket bought.

Code example:

  <p>
    <i>The London Eye</i> will be reopened from
    <b>
      <time datetime="2018-07-07">July 7</time>
    </b>.
  </p>

  <p>
    Opening hours start at
    <b>
      <time datetime="09:00">09:00<time>
    </b> and you'll
    be able to enjoy the view for at least
    <b>
      <time datetime="PT2H30M">1h 30m</time>
    </b> per ticket bought.
  </p>
