Some more infos about the hr & br tags

As I previously talked about these tags with my colleagues, we actually found a whole array of uses for the so said tags. But, you shouldn't abuse these tags.Therefore let's give some examples:

  1. Separate the lines of an address:

    1234 North East,
    Main Street Lagoon,
    Office Post 54555,
    Ireland

    Code: <address> 1234 North East, <br /> Main Street Lagoon, <br /> Office Post 54555,<br /> Ireland </address>

  2. Separate the lines of an address,

    using both tags:

    Location:
    1234 North East,
    Main Street Lagoon,
    Office Post 54555,
    Ireland

    Code: <address> Location: <hr /> 1234 North East, <br /> Main Street Lagoon, <br /> Office Post 54555,<br /> Ireland </address>

  3. Separation of sections of an article / content:

    Lorem Ih3sum
